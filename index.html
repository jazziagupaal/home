SAMPLE WORKS

Here's a glimpse of some of the projects I was involved in.

REVITALIZE

Revitalize is a calming open-world game designed to help those with anxiety. Players can explore beautiful, relaxing landscapes at their own pace while completing gentle quests.

TAYOG SKIRMISH

This chess-like game begins with players taking turns to place their first piece, a bird, until seven pieces are on the board. The game then starts and is available on mobile and as a physical board game.

TAYOG VOYAGERS

This tabletop game features each player with four birds and a deck of ability cards. It includes a physical board, miniature figures, and cards.

About Me
